About Me

I’m Ben Staunton, a Mathematics graduate from the University of Nottingham, now pursuing an MSc in Statistics at the University of Warwick. With years of tutoring experience, I specialise in guiding students through GCSE and A-Level Mathematics and Statistics, as well as supporting advanced learners preparing for university-level study.

My approach is structured yet personal: I first explain the underlying theory, then demonstrate with worked examples, and finally guide students as they apply their understanding through practice. This cycle builds confidence, sharpens problem-solving skills, and ensures lasting mastery.
